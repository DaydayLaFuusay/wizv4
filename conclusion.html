Key Takeaways:

From this comprehensive training, the following are the key takeaways:

  • 1 Comprehensive understanding of ISO/SAE 21434:2021 and its application in the automotive industry.
  • 2 Insight into the management of cybersecurity at an organisational level and in relation to specific projects.
  • 3 Knowledge about the concept development phase, product development phase, and post development phase of a product and their cybersecurity considerations.
  • 4 Understanding of the process of responding to cybersecurity incidents and validating cybersecurity measures.
  • 5 Insight into the ongoing activities required for maintaining cybersecurity and practical examples of implementing cybersecurity.
